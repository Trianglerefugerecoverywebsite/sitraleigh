I hope you all have had a pleasant transition into the new year.  

Tonight, 1/6/26 is our first session of the year. We will begin with the core practices of the dhamma and over the next months, work through the foundation of the Buddha's teaching.   Tonight's meditation and teaching will focus on the Dhammacakkappavattana Sutta, or Putting the Wheel of Dhamma in  motion. The Buddha gave this talk to five mendicants he had practiced with for many years. It was some seven weeks after his awakening at Bodhgaya.  After a very long walk he met his companions at the Deer Park in Sarnath.  One of the mostvmemorable phrases was this;   "Whatever has the nature to arise also has the nature to cease"
