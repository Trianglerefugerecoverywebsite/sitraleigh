Each week in our newsletter, we share a short teaching — a single idea to carry into practice. This blog is where that idea gets a little more room to breathe.

The hope is continuity. You might read the teaching in the newsletter on Monday, watch a brief reflection, come sit with us on Wednesday, and then find the fuller thought here — something to return to when the week gets loud.

There's nothing you need to do with these posts. Read them, skip them, come back to them. Like the practice itself, they're offered freely, and you're welcome exactly as you are.

With metta,
Cary
