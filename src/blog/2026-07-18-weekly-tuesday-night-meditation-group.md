**Join us Tuesday nights, sit with community and deepen your practice.**

**Sit Raleigh has a home for our WEEKLY Tuesday night meditation group.**

We  now meet at Greendoor Vitality.

This coming Tuesday, 10/28 the topic will be the concentration as described in the Satipatthana Sutta. I hope to see you there.

The address is **704 Glenwood Ave in Raleigh** (just past the intersection of Peace st. going north).   
The driveway is just before the house. As always, all are welcome.  


Meditation / talk (7-8pm) is by donation (dana) and the suggested donation is $10, 
however no one will be turned away - 

*everyone is welcome to attend - your generosity supports the community.*

We have cushions and chairs - feel free to bring your own.  

Free parking available in the rear or on the street.

Also we are hoping community members will consider an additional monthly donation to make sure we can keep this wonderful space . 

 GreenDoor Vitality is most generous, but the space isn't free.  email me if you are able to support the sangha in any way. 

 
for more info about the location and their other offerings visit www.greendoorvitality.com
