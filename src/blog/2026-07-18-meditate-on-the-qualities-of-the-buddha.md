**Monday December the 8th is Bodhi Day. This is the celebration of the Buddha's enlightenment.  Our Tuesday weekly group will meditate on the qualities of the Buddha and hear some Jataka tales, stories of the Buddha's past lives.  We will also enjoy milk rice, the food the Buddha was given by Sujata, which ended his asceticism, and giving him enough strength and nourishment to sit and become enlightened.**

**see you at 7pm Tuesday December 9th.**
