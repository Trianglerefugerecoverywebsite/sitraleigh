Is Nibbana or Nirvana possible?   Yes it is, absolutely.  But letting go of craving for things to be a certain way, doesn't mean we continually let it go. This can be steady and stay but nothing is guaranteed.  My understanding is this; it begins with a wise desire for the end of suffering about a particular thing or situation.  Then the intention to comprehend the nature and source of the suffering.  What do we want to be different or to not change?  Then begins the contemplation, what is beneath this, and we uncover and uncover. This may take a moment or a year, all depends on the individual situation. Eventually there is insight to the source of our difficulty. Once there is insight we can then begin the letting go, often just the understanding of the source frees us, but without an understanding then what are we actually letting go of?       This talk by Buddhist teacher Joseph Goldstein, is worth a listen or two.
