Against the Stream - Noah Levine.  Presenting the basics of Buddhism with personal anecdotes, exercises, and guided meditations and more.  Great book.[www.againstthestream.com/dharma-punx-merch/against-the-stream-book](https://www.againstthestream.com/dharma-punx-merch/against-the-stream-book) 

The Noble Eightfold Path by Bhikku Bodhi [www.accesstoinsight.org/lib/authors/bodhi/waytoend.html](https://www.accesstoinsight.org/lib/authors/bodhi/waytoend.html)   also available as a paperback

The Heart of the Buddha’s Teaching: Transforming Suffering into Peace, Joy, and Liberation. By Thich Nhat Hanh <https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/209574.The_Heart_of_the_Buddha_s_Teaching>

This website is an audio site with guided meditations to practice the entire Satipatthana Sutta.  Bhikkhu Analayo has also written a wonderful detailed exploration of the Sutta as well as a written guide to go along with the meditations.  This is not light reading, but I cannot recommend it high enough, [www.windhorsepublications.com/satipatthana-%20meditation-audio/](https://www.windhorsepublications.com/satipatthana-%20meditation-audio/)  

Being with Dying - Roshi Joan Halifax. Roshi Joan is a force of nature, if you are not familiar with her do yourself a favor and change that. This book is an unflinching look at death and dying and how to walk through it. There is a CD version where she reads it and guides meditations and a print version. Both are wonderful  [www.amazon.com/Being-Dying-Cultivating-Compassion-Fearlessness/dp/1590307186](https://www.amazon.com/Being-Dying-Cultivating-Compassion-Fearlessness/dp/1590307186)

The Bodhisattva's Embrace -Hozan Alan Senauke  A beautiful exploration of engaged Buddhism by a wonderful teacher.
